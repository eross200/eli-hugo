Hey, My name’s Eli!

I live in Southeast Michigan, and I’m into graphic design, specifically for the web. I do print, but lately my focus has been designing Bootstrap-based responsive websites, specifically using Content Management Systems like WordPress, and eCommerce platforms like Magento. I’d love to work with you! I encourage you to take a look around and check me out.

Skills:

I specialize in front-end website design, as well as print publications. I write HTML5 and CSS3 / Sass, a basic knowledge of Javascript and PHP, and am comfortable working with responsive front-end development frameworks like Bootstrap. I have experience with developing responsive themes for Wordpress, as well as the eCommerce platform Magento. I have extensive experience with multiple creative software applications, including the Adobe Creative Suite, specifically Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign.
